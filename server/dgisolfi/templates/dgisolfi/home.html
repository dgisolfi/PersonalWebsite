IEEE Published Paper

HoneyNet Environment for Analyzing Malicious Actors

Researched and developed honeypots which are web applications or resources that are deceptively constructed to log actions of its users, most (but not all) of whom can be assumed to be malicious actors. They allow for vast amounts of data to be collected for analysis. In a paper, my coworkers and I wrote and discussed how we came to build a network of honeypots, from design to implementation, and a few insights gained by analyzing attack data gathered from it. We presented our research at IEEE MIT Undergraduate Research Technology Conference which will be published to the IEEE Research Directory.
